Welcome to SINDy Module

Ever wish there was a more efficient way to find the hidden mathematical equations governing your experimental data?

The Problem Statement: If you have a bunch of raw data, but you don't know the exact differential equations or trajectory that these data follow, SINDy will solve this for you.

By using Sparse Identification of Nonlinear Dynamics (SINDy), the algorithm automatically sifts through a library of candidate functions to discover the sparsest, cleanest physical laws governing your system.
